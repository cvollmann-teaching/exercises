\textbf{Classical Gram--Schmidt Algorithm}\\
Let $A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$ be defined as 
$$
	A := \begin{pmatrix}
		3&4\\
		2&7
	\end{pmatrix}.
$$ 
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Compute a $QR$-decomposition of $A$ using the Gram-Schmidt algorithm.
	\item Compute $QR= A$ to check your result.
\end{enumerate}
