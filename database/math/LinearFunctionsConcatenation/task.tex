\textbf{Concatenation of Linear Functions}
 
 
 	Let $V_1,V_2, V_3$ be vector spaces and $f\colon V_1 \to V_2$ and $g\colon V_2 \to V_3$ be linear. Then the concatenation $g \circ f\colon V_1 \to V_3$ is linear.
