\textbf{Compute Gershgorin Disks}\\
Compute the Gershgorin disks of the following matrices and denote the matrices which necessarily have positive eigenvalues:
\label{ex:gershgorin_home}
\begin{align*}
A=\begin{pmatrix}
14&10&8\\
10&14&8\\
8&8&6
\end{pmatrix}, \hspace{.2cm}
B=\begin{pmatrix}
10&1&5\\
1&17&5\\
5&5&25
\end{pmatrix}, \hspace{.2cm}
C=\begin{pmatrix}
5&1&3\\
1&-2&-3\\
3&-3&-6
\end{pmatrix}.
\end{align*}
